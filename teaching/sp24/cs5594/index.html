Resources

The best source for this course is research articles that can be found on Google or public repositories such as iacr or arxiv. Some preferred books for cryptography and blockchain are as follows.

Blockchain Textbook
  • Bitcoin and Cryptocurrency Technologies. Arvind Narayanan, Joseph Bonneau, Edward Felten, Andrew Miller and Steven Goldfeder. Princeton University Press, 2016. (draft version here)
  • Foundations of Distributed Consensus and Blockchains (free). Elaine Shi. Book manuscript, 2020.
Crypto Textbook
  • Introduction to Modern Cryptography (3rd edition). Jonathan Katz and Yehuda Lindell. Chapman and Hall/CRC, 2020.
  • A Graduate Course in Applied Cryptography (free). Dan Boneh and Victor Shoup. Book manuscript, 2020
