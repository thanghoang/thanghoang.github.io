Short Bio

I am a PhD student at University of South Florida advised by Dr. Attila A. Yavuz. I am studying cryptography and machine learning, with special interests in privacy-enhancing technologies (i.e., Searchable Encryption, ORAM, Biometric Cryptosystems).
